Orgin of the Webserver

My cousin, a wonderful person, in the summer of 2020 gifted me a hardrive for my laptop that had linux on it. He also taught me some basic skills and encouraged me to try some of these things. I took a while to get around to it, but I finally made a webserver routed through a raspberry pi. This action was also motivated by the extra donation he promised to my High School Robotics Team.
