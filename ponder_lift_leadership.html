Contemplation, Abstraction, and Theorising on the Qualities of Leaders

What is a leader?

Wikipedia defines Leadership as:

"Leadership, both as a research area and as a practical skill, encompasses the ability of an individual, group or organization to "lead", influence or guide other individuals, teams, or entire organizations."
However, what does leadership really mean?

Leadership in some eyes is seen as easy. Like one could simply peform it as if riding a bike: Hard at first or if it has been a while, but easy once you get going. I believe that is a misguided view of how life works. Life is rarely so simple, with its hundreds of shades of grey, white, and black.

Let's get some facts out of the way:

  1. A Good Leader can have a bad day
  2. A Bad Leader Can have a good day
  3. Judgement of leader should change
  4. People can change and have changed
  5. Never discount another's problems unless they do not seek change

These should be the principles expected of all. One should not mistake a few competent choices or actions for a good leader.A Good leader should try to always help their people. They should also believe in an end goal or something. They don't need to have extremely well laid plans. A leader should be able to change to the situation quickly. To quote WW1 German General Luddendorf: "Punch a hole and let the rest follow." You shouldn't commit to plans that must be followed down to the letter. Have a good idea of an endgoal, but be willing to change. And take this change and adapt.
