Why Should People Believe I should Care?

In Food service, there is a Phrase thrown around that basically declares the customer to be supreme. Well I believe the Customer is full of bunk. There are many customers who are just fine and in fact, are super cool. But the ones who believe they hold all the power.


One lady, one time, yelled at me about a mistake the cashier made regarding Tomatos. I had not been informed of a Tomato Allergy and the lady was actually in the wrong. I did care, and when informed,I ordered my coworker on the line to change knives and gloves. But this lady yelled at me and eventually asked me for my name. I gave her a fake name, to get her out of the store.


Those kinds of people are simply seeking attention. To make me quiver in my boots and bow down to them. Because to them, they believe that all managers and bosses are afraid of them. That the Customer is supreme. In some cases, to bend the knee is the only way to keep an employee. But most employees, if they work hard and present themselves as a good honest worker to their boss should have nothing to fear.


This customer is a type of ages old parasite: The shirker of duty and work. For a good person is not the one who goes to Church and hears of the golden rule, then tries to blackmail the workers into giving them free food. The good person is the one who treats all mistakes as all that, mistakes. For no one learns from a mistake when they are unable to improve.
